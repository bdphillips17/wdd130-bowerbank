Library Resources

Races

Races determine the ancestry of your character. Key information includes origin traits, movement speed, and natural abilities. Examples include Human, Elf, Dwarf, and Dragonborn.

Classes

Your class is your primary profession and skill set. Key information includes hit dice, primary abilities, saving throws, and armor proficiencies. Examples include Fighter, Wizard, Rogue, and Cleric.

Feats

Feats represent special talents or areas of expertise that give a character special capabilities. They can replace ability score improvements and provide powerful combat or utility options.

Backgrounds

Backgrounds reveal where your character came from, how they became an adventurer, and their place in the world. Key information includes skill proficiencies, starting equipment, and background features.

Creatures

Creatures and monsters you will encounter in your travels. Key information includes Challenge Rating (CR), hit points, armor class, and unique actions or legendary resistances.
