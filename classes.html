Fighter

Source: Player's Handbook (2024 Revised)
Requirement: None

Benefit: A master of martial combat, skilled with a variety of weapons and armor. Fighters learn the basics of all combat styles. Every fighter can heft an axe, fence with a rapier, wield a longsword or a greatsword, use a bow, and even trap foes in a net with some degree of skill.
